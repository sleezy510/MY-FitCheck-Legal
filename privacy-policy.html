My FitCheck Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

My FitCheck ("we", "us", "our") provides AI-powered virtual try-on and motion preview tools. This Privacy Policy explains what information we process and how we use it.

Information We Process

  • Photos you upload for body, clothing, shoes, and accessories.
  • Generated outputs such as try-on images and motion clips.
  • Basic account details when you sign in (for example Apple/Google profile data).
  • Subscription and entitlement state used to manage in-app features.
  • Technical diagnostics and logs needed to keep the service reliable.

How We Use Information

  • Generate AI try-on and motion outputs.
  • Save your wardrobe/preferences and improve app reliability.
  • Support purchases, subscriptions, and usage limits.

Third-Party Processing

To deliver core AI functionality, certain uploads and metadata may be processed by service providers, including AI inference and identity/payment providers.

Data Retention

We retain data only as needed for app functionality, user support, legal obligations, and service integrity.

Your Choices

  • You can remove local wardrobe/history items in-app.
  • You can stop using the service at any time.
  • You can request support for account/data questions at the contact below.

Children

My FitCheck is not directed to children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: privacy@myfitcheck.app
